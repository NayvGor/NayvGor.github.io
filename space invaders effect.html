About

Space Invaders Effect is a VR remake of the frenzy version of the famous arcade game. It was made on Unity for a school project in team of 4 : Axel Marcial, Lucas Estingoy, Oihan Desperben and myself. I developped the laser system and explosion VFX.
This game gaves to us the opportunity to go to Laval Virtual in 2024, Europe’s biggest XR event, and at the same time to participate in the hackathon.
During this competition we developed a Mölkky in MR (Mixed Reality).
